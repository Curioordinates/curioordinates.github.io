UK Haunted Hotels

Welcome to my on-going project to categorise haunted hotels in the UK, aimed at encouraging people to explore the country and perhaps learn something of our haunted heritage, and our rich tapestry of history. Please visit regularly for frequent updates!
If you don't see a famous hotel in my listing, its because of work I am doing on my ghost survey, where I have been contacting thousands of venues asking about their current "haunted" status and whether they have experienced anything paranormal in the last few years. I felt it approrpriate to omit locations that have reported nothing - it saves you time and money if you are planning to stay at the hotel. I apologise for the lack of hotels (to date) in Northern Ireland. There main candidate was the Ballygally Castle - but they informed me that all has been quiet for a while now.

Please note that I can't be held liable for the non-appearance of any ghosts or associated phenomena; or even if room numbers have changed since my write-up! Most of the hotels below are unresponsive to any ghost reports, so if you do experience something, please email me here

Flitwick Manor Hotel
Church Road, Flitwick, MK45 1AE
An old Victorian lady in white, a little hazy but otherwise solid, has been seen walking through various rooms and along corridors at night, looking out of the windows; she has also been seen in the upstairs bedrooms where she has been seen crying while seated on the bed. Staff have reported feeling "a cool hand" on their forehead and a heavy weight on their beds during the night. Interestingly, the hauntings intensified after a small room, probably used by servants, was found in the loft. There is also a story of a little girl who was seen by a cleaner, who had felt something touch her skirt. The cleaner left the next day!
