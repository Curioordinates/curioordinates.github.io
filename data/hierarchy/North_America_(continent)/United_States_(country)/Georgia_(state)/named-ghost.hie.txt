32.076941048236264, -81.08878522785506`Anne Powell`https://www.ghostsandgravestones.com/savannah/haunted-places-in-georgia`At the 17 Hundred 90 Inn & Tavern, the ghost of Anne Powell—a 17-year-old girl said to have fallen to her death from room 204—reportedly lingers, especially around guests who stay in that room. Visitors describe sightings of Anne at the bedside, gentle touches, missing belongings, and emotional apparitions, making the inn one of Savannah’s most famously haunted stays.
